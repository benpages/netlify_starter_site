About

whatifididnt is a blog about alternative lifestyles.

Started in 2022, it is a popular destination for people looking to explore different ways of living.

It features articles about everything from simple tweaks you can make to your life to completely transforming it. You'll also find interviews with people who have chosen interesting paths.

whatifididnt is a safe space where you can pick and choose what works for you and design your dream lifestyle—without judgement.
